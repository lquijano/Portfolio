Welcome, to LQ's website!

How exciting! I get to talk about myself in third person!

Leslie is an ENTJ, adventure seeking, web developer - in that order.

Originally from Bell, Ca in Los Angeles. I moved up to the Bay Area to begin my academic journey at UC Berkeley. I completed my Bachelor of Arts in Political Economy and moved to San Franciso to work in Finance. After three wonderful busy seasons with PricewatehouseCoopers LLC. I decided to re-enroll at Cal, to attain a certificate in Full Stack Web Developement. Here we are now.

I enjoy making cards, traveling, yoga, hiking, and cooking.
